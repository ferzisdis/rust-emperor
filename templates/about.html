Dark Emperor is a medieval strategy and resource management game where you start as a Baron or Baroness and work your way up to become Emperor or Empress.

Manage your resources wisely, build structures, maintain your army, keep your citizens happy, and navigate through random events to achieve the ultimate rank!

üéØ How to Play

  • Resources: Manage Gold, Food, Citizens, Soldiers, and more
  • Buildings: Construct Farms, Mines, Smithies, Markets, and upgrade your Castle
  • Settings: Adjust Taxes and Food Rations to balance income and popularity
  • Advancement: Meet requirements to advance through 5 noble ranks
  • Time Limit: Achieve Emperor/Empress status before the year 1500

üëë Noble Ranks

1. Baron/Baroness
2. Count/Countess
3. Duke/Duchess
4. Prince/Princess
5. King/Queen
6. Emperor/Empress
